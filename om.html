What is I?

I don't know who I am. I am not certain at least. So far, I've gathered that I am a conscious human. I think that qualifies me as a "person." That means I've got a "personality." That part I haven't figured out yet. It's fluid. I like to think it's a good to some degree. A good man with a good heart, I think is fair to say.

But since I am a person, I am defined by what I do, especially as a man. So what do I do? At the moment, I am studying English in Copenhagen. That's the easy part. But studying that is merely a reason, or excuse, to do things I actually like and call it sensible. I like words and language. I like stories and aesthetics. I like things that make me feel and think.

They say your home is a reflection of your mind. Well, if you look at this website's homepage, that's what you'll find. My mind. Categorically divided into different fields of interest. Writing, poetry, visual arts, music. That's what I'd say I am. Those things.

But if you want to get more intimate than that, then here are directions: read my poetry (aloud) and listen to this playlist (it's rather long). That is I.
